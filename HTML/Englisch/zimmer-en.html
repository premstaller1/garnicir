Cancellation policy

In case of a cancellation:

  • Up to 90 days before departure can be canceled free of charge.
  • between 90 and 15 days before the arrival date, the deposit of 30% of the total price will be retained.
  • between 14 days and 7 days before the arrival date, 60% of the agreed total price will be charged.
  • late arrival or early departure, 100% of the agreed total price will be charged.

The cancellation must be written and must be received by the hotel no later than the above mentioned date.
