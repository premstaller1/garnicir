About us

History and tradition in one house.

Garni Cir is a family owned hotel in the hearth of Val Gardena, Italy. Already in thrid generation, we, the family Rabanser, are trying to make your vacation in Santa Christina as good as possible.

Here you can find everything your heart can desire - from the breathtaking view on top of the Langkofel, a ski trip around the dolomites, or a quick trip to Bolzano, the capital of southtyrol.
